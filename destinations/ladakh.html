About Ladakh

Ladakh is a land of stark, breathtaking beauty — where turquoise lakes shimmer against barren mountains, ancient monasteries cling to cliffsides, and the sky feels impossibly close. From the iconic Pangong Tso Lake and the sand dunes of Nubra Valley to the magnetic Khardung La Pass and the spiritual Leh city, every corner of Ladakh is a photographer's dream and an adventurer's paradise. With its unique Tibetan-Buddhist culture and some of the world's most dramatic high-altitude landscapes, Ladakh is truly unlike anywhere else on earth.
