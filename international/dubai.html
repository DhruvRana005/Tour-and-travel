About Dubai

Dubai is a city of superlatives — the world's tallest building, the largest mall, the most luxurious hotels. Yet beyond the glittering skyline lies a rich Arabian heritage of desert dunes, traditional souks, and warm hospitality. From thrilling desert safaris and dhow cruises to world-class shopping and fine dining, Dubai offers an experience that seamlessly blends the ancient and the ultra-modern. And with just a 3-hour flight from India, it's one of the most accessible international destinations.
